About me

My name is Syafiq Bin Abdul Samad, and I’m 20 years old. Born on 11 October, I am currently pursuing a diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Segamat.

I am the third of five siblings in my family, and I deeply value the strong bond I share with my parents and siblings. My parents, Abdul Samad Bin Yusof and Norehan Binti Abdullah, have always been my pillars of support and inspiration.

My ambition is to become a wonderlust, and I live by the saying, "the world is a book, and those who do not travel read only one page." Another quote that drives me is from Zlatan Ibrahimović: "I don’t need a trophy to tell myself I’m the best." To me, this means success is not defined by accolades or external validation. Traveling the world is my dream, and I trust in my determination to make it a reality.

To achieve this goal and succeed in life, I believe in these core values:

  • A Good heart
  • A Clear Vision
  • Supportive Parents
